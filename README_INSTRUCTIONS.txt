Requirements
- Downloaded Sound Bank (Provided within Jar just in case)
- JRE of 8 or higher.
- JSE of 14 or higher.


Open the Jar Executable by double clicking on it.

Press "Play" to start the live improvisation.

Press "Pause" to pause the live improvisation.

Press "Reset" to go back to the beginning of your musical piece.

Press "Save Created Song" to save the music generated into a MIDI file.

Click the drop down menu to select the theme/scale you'd like from "Major", "Minor" and "Blues"
